Growing up I watched UFC and MMA with my dad. I love watching combat sports and love the atmosphere it creates in the crowds around the ring. I have a favorite boxer, but he is not in the UFC or MMA. He is a One Champion fighter, named Rodtang Jitmuangnon.

I recently got into boxing on my own. I think Muay Thai is really fun and interesting, this is mostly influenced by my favorite fighter, Rodtang. My friend who is a skilled kickboxer teaches me behind the Science Building in the evenings. We have a lot of fun sparring and conversating. Boxing brings me a lot of joy.

When Boxing I make sure to pack the following in my duffle bag:

  1. Boxing Gloves
  2. Water Bottle
  3. Shin Protectors
  4. Mouth Guard

Rodtang said the following:

"In all sports, you have victories and defeats. Muay Thai is a sport that teaches us to learn and to grow as people. It shows us what it means to experience both victory and defeat, and teaches us how to forgive".

-Rodtang Jitmuangnon

All in all, I'd like to say I love to watch and experience boxing more than I like to breath & eat sometimes (this is an exaggeration, of course)

Boxing > Breathing
