Rodtang Jitmuangnon a.k.a Muay Thai Iron Man

Rodtang's History

Born in 1997, Rodtang is a proffesional Muay Thai and kickboxer. He fights under his famous nickname "Iron Man" given to him due to his ability to withstand heavy blows and give them just the same. He began fighting at the age of 7 and became an official One Champion fighter following a series of wins in 2017 leading to his signing on September 22, 2018. Rodtang continues to fight in One Champion battle, winning his most recent match against Takeru Segawa via. knockout in the first round.

Rodtang Now

Rodtang is now married to Aida Looksaikongdin and they recently had a baby boy Zlatan. Through their marriage, Rodtang also converted to the Islamic faith. During his recent fight with Takeru, he had just completed Ramadan and accreditted a large part of his win to his new-found faith.
