Expalin the difference between an absolut link and a relative link. Why would you use relative links?

an absolute link leads your user to an outside source from your website; it has a different domain. Your relative link would bea link to your own source; it has the same domain. Relative links are more convinent and if you change your domain it would still be easily accessible.
